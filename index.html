Enhancing Cash Flow through Improved Eligibility & Pre-Authorization

Background

  • A well-established dental practice in Austin, TX, had been experiencing challenges with cash flow due to delays and denials in insurance claims.
  • The practice struggled with managing eligibility checks and obtaining pre-authorizations, which impacted their revenue cycle and patient satisfaction.
  • The practice was keen on improving these processes to ensure timely reimbursement and optimize cash flow.
  • Location Austin, TX
  • Practice Type: General Dentistry
  • Number of Locations: 2
  • Number of Providers: 5
  • Patient Volume: 1,200 patients per month

Challenges

  • Delayed Reimbursements: Frequent delays in insurance claim approvals led to delayed payments and inconsistent cash flow.
  • High Denial Rates: Claims were often denied due to issues with eligibility verification and missing pre-authorizations, resulting in additional administrative work and lost revenue.
  • Administrative Burden: The in-house team spent significant time verifying patient eligibility and securing pre-authorizations, which detracted from patient care and practice management.
  • Patient Satisfaction: Inefficiencies in the eligibility and pre-authorization process occasionally led to delays in treatment, negatively impacting patient satisfaction.

Solution

The Clinic partnered with CEC to leverage our comprehensive Eligibility & Pre-Authorization services. Our solution focused on the following key areas:

  • Eligibility Verification: Implementing automated systems to quickly and accurately verify patient eligibility before appointments. This included real-time eligibility checks to ensure that insurance information was up-to-date and valid.
  • Pre-Authorization Management: Streamlining the pre-authorization process by handling all pre-authorization requests and follow-ups. This ensured that required approvals were obtained promptly, reducing the likelihood of claim denials.
  • Staff Training: Providing training for the practice’s administrative staff on best practices for managing eligibility and pre-authorization processes, improving their efficiency and effectiveness.
  • Dedicated Support: Assigning a dedicated team to manage and oversee the eligibility and pre-authorization processes, offering expertise and support to resolve any issues that arose.

Results

  • Improved Cash Flow: By implementing our eligibility and pre-authorization services, Dental Clinic saw a significant improvement in their cash flow. The time from service delivery to payment decreased by 30%, leading to a more predictable and stable revenue stream.
  • Reduced Claim Denials: The rate of claim denials dropped by 40% due to accurate eligibility verification and timely pre-authorizations. This reduction in denials led to a higher rate of successful reimbursements and less administrative work to resolve issues.
  • Enhanced Efficiency: The practice’s administrative staff reported a 50% reduction in the time spent on eligibility and pre-authorization tasks, allowing them to focus more on patient care and other critical areas of the practice.
  • Increased Patient Satisfaction: With more streamlined processes and fewer delays, patients experienced fewer disruptions in their treatment plans, leading to higher satisfaction scores and improved patient retention.

Conclusion

The collaboration between Dental Clinic and CEC demonstrates the value of specialized Eligibility & Pre-Authorization services in enhancing practice efficiency and financial performance. By addressing key challenges and implementing effective solutions, CEC helped the clinic achieve improved cash flow, reduced claim denials, and increased patient satisfaction.
